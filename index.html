ABOUT SUO GUNSHOP

Founded in 1995, Suo GunShop has grown from a small family-owned business to one of the most respected firearms retailers in the industry. Our commitment to quality, customer service, and responsible gun ownership has earned us a loyal following among law enforcement, military personnel, and civilian shooters alike.

Our team consists of experienced firearms experts, many of whom are former military or law enforcement professionals. We personally test and evaluate every product we sell to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

At Suo GunShop, we believe in the right to bear arms responsibly. We conduct thorough background checks on all firearm purchases and offer free safety courses with every new firearm purchase.
